Purpose & vision
The next chapter of philanthropy must be more imaginative, more connected, and more human-centered.
And more optimistic.

We help organizations and individuals across the philanthropic ecosystem amplify their impact through strategic collaboration, storytelling, and systems design, bringing the same rigor and imagination to generosity that other sectors bring to growth.

Who we work with

Built for the full spectrum of giving.

Philanthropy Lab works with major foundations and philanthropic investors, grassroots NGOs, and family and emerging philanthropists. We're a good fit whether you're a grantmaker rethinking how capital moves, a family exploring values-aligned giving for the first time, or an NGO looking to turn your impact into a story that builds a coalition around it.

What we do

Four ways we work with you.

Curiosity & Creativity Development

Innovation labs and design sprints exploring new models of capital deployment, trust-based philanthropy, and future-of-giving trends. We run strategic ideation frameworks to help clients identify bold moonshot ideas and cross-sector experiments, plus AI readiness and adaptability support.

Collaboration Strategy

Support developing coalitions, co-funding partnerships, and collective impact models, including facilitation of ecosystem mapping to identify potential collaborators, aligned capital, and catalytic funding opportunities.

Community Building

Design of fellowships, peer networks, and donor engagement experiences for both institutional and individual givers, along with specialized and inspiring convenings and journeys that foster alignment among funders, grantees, and ecosystem partners.

Content & Storytelling

Strategy and production support for impact narratives, case studies, digital series, and annual reports, plus messaging development for philanthropic campaigns, investment theses, and public influence strategies.

Key initiatives

Signature programs shaping how we work.

For Organizations

Structured engagements for foundations, nonprofits, and funders ready to move from strategy on paper to strategy in motion.

Strategic Clarity & Messaging

Most organizations can list everything they do. Few can say the one thing that makes them essential. We work with your team to find that thing: the core story that explains your value in language funders, boards, and partners actually respond to. Then we translate it into materials your organization can use immediately.

Discovery & Strategy Design

For organizations entering a real strategic shift (a new agenda, a new theory of change, a next chapter), we run a structured discovery process: stakeholder conversations, synthesis, and a clear set of recommendations. Phase 0 is deliberately scoped and short, so you get direction before you commit to a full strategy build.

Board & Leadership Activation

A retreat should produce conviction, not just consensus. We design board retreats and leadership convenings that turn strategic work your team has already done into something your board feels ownership over, leaving the room ready to act, not just ready to nod.

Place-Based Initiative Design

Regional initiatives focused on helping funders and NGOs collaborate around shared geographies and civic priorities.

For Donors and Grantmakers

Focused engagements for individuals and families navigating giving, legacy, and the systems that carry them out.

Family Giving Design

Bespoke strategy sessions for multi-generational families exploring values-aligned philanthropy and legacy storytelling.

Funder + Grantee Experience Audits

Tools and coaching to help grantmakers redesign their internal systems for equity, efficiency, and empathy.
