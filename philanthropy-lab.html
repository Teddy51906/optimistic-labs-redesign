Purpose & vision

The next chapter of philanthropy must be more imaginative, more connected, more human-centered, and more optimistic.

We help organizations and individuals across the philanthropic ecosystem amplify their impact through strategic collaboration, storytelling, and systems design, bringing the same rigor and imagination to generosity that other sectors bring to growth.

Philanthropy Lab is built for the full spectrum of giving: major foundations and philanthropic investors, grassroots NGOs, and family and emerging philanthropists. We're a good fit whether you're a grantmaker rethinking how capital moves, a family exploring values-aligned giving for the first time, or an NGO looking to turn your impact into a story that builds a coalition around it.
