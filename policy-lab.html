Optimistic Labs | Policy

Where policy and civic imagination meet strategic influence.

Policy Lab is an impact-focused strategy studio and activation engine for institutions, associations, nonprofits, and public affairs teams working to restore civic trust and advance policy through connection, clarity, and creativity.

Book a conversation
Why it matters
Trust is the cornerstone of policy-making and democracy, and it's under pressure.

Those working in public policy and civic life are being asked to do more, faster, with fewer shared spaces, fewer common stories, and greater scrutiny. The result is fragmented strategies, disconnected stakeholders, and a diminished belief in what's possible.

Influence today isn't earned through effort alone. It's earned through voice, relationships, and narrative design. Policy Lab exists for institutions and leaders who want to build differently, helping stakeholders across the policy influence ecosystem move beyond talking points and position papers toward network-centered frameworks for influence, narrative, and collaboration that reflect both substance and story.

We are not a lobbying firm. We are not a messaging consultant. We're the connective tissue between trust, storytelling, and public action.

Who we work with

Built for the institutions shaping public trust.

Policy Lab works with a diverse array of public-facing institutions and influence ecosystems, including:

  • National and regional associations navigating generational change, member trust, and evolving civic mandates
  • Civic and policy nonprofits advancing new ideas or field-wide frameworks for social, economic, or democratic innovation
  • Corporate public affairs and social responsibility teams aligning civic strategy with internal values and public-facing messaging
  • Think tanks and educational centers building bridges between research, community experience, and policy pathways
  • Civic funders and democracy advocates funding narrative change, civic innovation, or infrastructure for democratic resilience

We also work with coalitions, mayors' offices, cultural institutions, and cross-sector intermediaries looking to refresh their civic engagement strategy.

What we do

Four ways we work with you.

Policy Lab engagements are structured as multi-month partnerships: we serve as fractional experts and advisors, balancing deep listening with strategic precision to clarify voice, grow community, and mobilize alignment across your stakeholders.

Narrative Strategy & Public Positioning

Multi-month partnership

We help organizations develop clear, emotionally intelligent messaging that speaks across stakeholder segments and political divides, including message platform development, values framing, and strategic communications support for campaigns, testimony, and institutional voice.

Stakeholder Engagement & Community Activation

Multi-month partnership

We design experiences, both digital and in-person, that bring together unusual allies and elevate public participation: salons, convenings, public engagement strategies, and co-learning spaces between government, private, and community sectors.

Coalition & Partnership Architecture

Multi-month partnership

We support the development of aligned initiatives across public, nonprofit, and corporate sectors, building or refreshing multi-organization coalitions, national initiatives, and cross-functional working groups through facilitation, shared language tools, and ecosystem design.

Field Strategy & Civic Infrastructure Design

Multi-month partnership

We advise civic funders, associations, and field leaders on how to shape or scale long-term civic infrastructure, including democracy movement analysis, trust-based governance strategy, and public narrative campaigns aligned with cultural, political, and geographic context.

All Policy Lab work is grounded in respect for diverse perspectives, attention to civic nuance, and a commitment to designing systems that invite, not dictate, public imagination.
