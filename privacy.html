Privacy Policy

Effective July 14, 2026

Who we are

Optimistic Labs ("we," "us") helps organizations design and build communities. This policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and the choices you have. Questions? Email us at hello@optimisticlabs.com.

Information you give us

  • Contact form. If you send us a note, we receive the name, email address, and message you provide, and we use them to respond to you.
  • Newsletter. If you subscribe, your email address is collected and managed through our newsletter provider so we can send you the newsletter.
  • Booking a call. If you book a call, your details are handled by email or by our scheduling provider so we can hold the meeting.

Information collected automatically

We do not run our own analytics and we do not set our own cookies. Like most websites, some technical information (such as your IP address and browser type) is processed by the third-party services that make this site work:

  • Hosting. Our web host may keep standard server logs.
  • Fonts. Typefaces are served by Google Fonts, which receives your IP address when the fonts load.
  • Newsletter embeds. The newsletter feed and signup form on our homepage are provided by Supascribe.

Each of these providers handles data under its own privacy policy.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your messages and inquiries
  • To send you the newsletter you subscribed to
  • To schedule and hold calls you request
  • To operate, secure, and improve the website

How we share it

We never sell your personal information. We share it only with the service providers named above (hosting, fonts, newsletter, and scheduling), when required by law, or with your consent.

How long we keep it

We keep correspondence and subscriber information only as long as needed for the purposes above — for example, until you unsubscribe from the newsletter or ask us to delete your information.

Your choices and rights

  • You can unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time using the link in any email.
  • You can ask us to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you by emailing hello@optimisticlabs.com.
  • Depending on where you live (for example, the EU/UK or California), you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA. We honor valid requests under those laws.

Children

This website is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect the information we hold, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update this page and revise the effective date above.
